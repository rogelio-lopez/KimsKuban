A Taste of Cuba & Key West at Kim's Kuban

Kim's Kuban has been a local favorite in Key West since 1989. We're a family-owned business serving up authentic Cuban coffee and delicious Cuban sandwiches alongside other island favorites.

Our story began with a passion for sharing the vibrant flavors of Cuban cuisine. We use only the freshest ingredients to craft our classic Cubano sandwiches, savory empanadas, and steaming cups of Cuban coffee.

Craving a taste of Mexico alongside your Cuban classics? We've got you covered! We also offer a selection of popular Mexican dishes like tacos, burritos, and quesadillas.

Whether you're a local resident or a visitor soaking up the Key West charm, Kim's Kuban is your spot for a warm welcome, delicious food, and a taste of the islands.
